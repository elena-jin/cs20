Join Our Team

We’re looking for passionate, friendly, and dedicated people to join our growing family at Sink. If you love great coffee and an energetic team environment, we’d love to meet you!

Barista

Responsibilities include preparing espresso drinks, serving pastries, and maintaining a welcoming atmosphere for customers.

Schedule: Full-time or Part-time

Baker

Prepare daily pastries, cakes, and desserts for display. Experience in baking or culinary school is preferred.

Schedule: Early morning shifts, 5 days/week

Store Manager

Lead and support our café team, ensure quality service, and manage daily operations including inventory and scheduling.

Schedule: Full-time, mornings and weekends

Cashier

Provide friendly customer service, take orders accurately, and assist with daily café tasks.

Schedule: Part-time, flexible hours
