Catering Services

We provide coffee catering for corporate meetings, campus events, weddings, and private parties. From pour-over bars to espresso stations and pastry platters, our team brings a full café experience to your venue.

What we offer:

  • Espresso bar with barista (machine + grinder on site)
  • Batch-brew coffee & hot tea service
  • Cold brew & specialty drinks (seasonal)
  • Pastry platters and light bites
  • Onsite setup, cups, napkins, and cleanup

Tell us about your event and we’ll send a custom quote within one business day.
